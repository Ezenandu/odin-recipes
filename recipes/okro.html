Okro Soup


Description

Okro is well known food in western Africa and some parts of Asia. It is a vegetable, rich in vitamins and minerals. Enjoyed with swallow and a chilled drink. It's best eaten in the afternoon or evening to revitalize the body for/after a days job.

Ingredients

  • Red Oil
  • Water
  • pepper
  • Salt
  • Maggie
  • Diced okro
  • Cray fish
  • Goat Meat or Beef
  • Dry fish
  • Ogbono
  • Ugu leaf
  • chopped cow liver
  • Onion
  • Garri

Steps

  1. Boil meat with sufficient water for 30mins
  2. Add dry fish and allow to boil
  3. Add chopped cow liver
  4. Add blended Onion, pepper and cray fish and allow to boil
  5. Add maggie
  6. add Salt
  7. Add diced Okro and steam for 10mins
  8. Add Ugu and steam for 5mins
  9. Prepare Garri to be served along side the soup
